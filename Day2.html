facts about Radha and Krishna:

Radha and Krishna are one

Some consider Radha to be the feminine form of Krishna, and the two are often depicted together.

Radha is the supreme goddess

Some believe that Radha controls Krishna with her love, and that she is the supreme goddess of all.

Krishna's love for Radha is cosmic energy

The love between Radha and Krishna is thought to be a powerful cosmic energy that can create and maintain the universe, and help people get closer to the divine.

Krishna has 108 names

Like Shiva, Krishna has 108 names, including Gopal, Govind, Shyam, Ghanshyam, Mohan, Manohar, Girdhari, Hari, Baanke Bihari, Nandlala, Devakinandan, Krishna, Kishan, and Kanhaiya.
